Affirmation and Stereotype Threat

My values from week 1 were: Courage, Challenge, Leadership, Competence. I stand by these values and still think they are the most important to me. I also think that they can greatly help me mediate stereotype threats. I believe greatly in leading by example. I hope to apply an open minded, caring attitude to DBC and my future work place to create an inviting environment where people don’t feel judged by any stereotypes. I think it will take leadership and courage to confront those around me that are enforcing stereotypes or judging people for stereotypes. As far as exercises for when I am feeling down: I plan on reading ‘Happiness is a Habit’ throughout the summer and I hope I can pick up some healthy habits for improving my mood in times of stress or sadness.
